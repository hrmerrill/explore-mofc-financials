About This Dashboard

Disclaimer: This is a personal project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the Mid-Ohio Food Collective (MOFC). All financial figures are extracted programmatically from publicly available audits and IRS filings and have not been verified by MOFC. Use at your own discretion.

This dashboard was built to visualize the financial data published by the Mid-Ohio Food Collective.

Why

I was curious where I could contribute. I hypothesized I could save costs by, e.g., creating an open-source route optimization tool to reduce transportation costs, but I did not know if this was the most impactful contribution I could make. To identify where I can make the biggest impact, I needed to first understand this data.

What's Missing?

Granularity. MOFC serves many communities, and visualizing community-specific impact may reveal opportunities that are not visible at the overall level.

Who

Hunter Merrill: Github | LinkedIn | CV

Data Source

All data is extracted from MOFC's publicly available IRS Form 990 filings and annual audit reports (fiscal years 2019–2024). Each fiscal year runs July 1 – June 30; for example, "FY 2024" covers July 2023 through June 2024. 990 PDFs were processed via OCR and manually verified; audit PDFs are text-based and extracted programmatically. There still may be errors! If so, please let me know.

Feedback & Corrections

This is a collaborative project meant to support and uplift MOFC's mission. If you spot a data error, have suggestions, or want to contribute, please open an issue on GitHub. All corrections, feedback, and suggestions are welcome.
